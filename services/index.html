Leading Logistics Company

DPDgroup is the international network bringing together DPD, Chronopost, SEUR and BRT. We offer smart, efficient and convenient services in our local markets and around the world.
Our products and services bring together our local and international expertise – all designed with our customers, and their customers in mind.
As delivery experts, we value agility and so do our customers. We never stop evolving to create brilliantly simple solutions.
To that end, we’re setting new standards for delivery by expanding our two signature services across Europe:

IMPORT SERVICES

With our import services, you can import directly from your international suppliers. Arrange your inbound delivery through the personalized Import Services we provide. DPDgroup also provides tailor-made packaging services and transport solutions for companies within the technology sector.

EXPORT SERVICES

Our expertise in the medical courier services allows us to offer personalized healthcare logistics solutions to hospitals, laboratories and pharmaceutical industries. We understand the extreme sensitivity and the time critical nature of the shipments they carry.

AIR FREIGHT SERVICES

Our Air Cargo Service has the capacity to handle all types of cargoes and isn’t limited to size. Irrespective of where you want to ship your item to, DPDgroup Air Freight Services has what it takes to get it there fast.

 

 

SEA FREIGHT SERVICES

DPDgroup sea freight services are a better option when costs are your primary concern, time isn’t a factor or your shipments are large & heavy. We ship significant volumes yearly, enabling us to offer you capacity, frequent departures & competitive rates.
